ABOUT US!

With the continuous inflation within our nation, we hope to target some problems to reduce the economic crisis. Rodents are a factor to small businesses going into bankruptcy causing economic downfall. Alongside the economic impact, rodents are able to cause social impact to one’s health leading to a detrating health crisis. We hope our project would ease up these ongoing issues while spreading awareness to residents of New York City.

Our Mission

Quality of Life

Rat infestation is considered a quality of life index because it contains key components such as economic and health concerns. As the issue damages our economy, it will lead to noticeable negative impacts, including business bankruptcies and an increase in homelessness. Homelessness and rat-borne diseases are a factor in health concerns for individuals, potentially resulting in death.

Brighter Future

Research has shown how our society has taken action toward addressing the ongoing issue by implementing laws regarding waste disposal and establishing a daily sanitation schedule.
