ABOUT US!

With the continuous inflation within our nation, we hope to target some problems to reduce the economic crisis. Alongside the economic impact, rodents are able to cause social impact to one’s health leading to a detrating health crisis. Our Mission

Quality of Life

Rat infestation is considered a quality of life index because it contains key components such as economic and health concerns. As the issue damages our economy, it will lead to noticeable negative impacts, including business bankruptcies and an increase in homelessness. Homelessness and rat-borne diseases are a factor in health concerns for individuals, potentially resulting in death.

Brighter Future

Research has shown how our society has taken action toward addressing the ongoing issue by implementing laws regarding waste disposal and establishing a daily sanitation schedule.

Familiarity of Residents’ Knowledge on Law Regulations Regarding Rats


According to the data chart shown above, approximately 24.6% of respondents are either familiar or very familiar with the law regulations regarding trash disposal in NYC, while 7.8% of respondents are unsure about the law regulations. Furthermore, 67.6% respondents are either not at all familiar or only somewhat familiar with the law regulations regarding trash disposal in NYC; thus, more than half of the respondents are not familiar with the law regulations. As a result of our data, it can be inferred that residents who aren’t familiar with the law regulations may be unaware of waste disposal needing to be inside a tied plastic bag or placed in a trash can with a lid.

According to our chart above, 22.5% of respondents either are willing or very willing to join programs that are involved in getting rid of rats, while approximately 25.5% of respondents are somewhat willing to join.
